## The Challenge of Coastal Deck Maintenance

Coastal environments are notoriously aggressive. Salt air can accelerate the corrosion of metal flashing, while intense UV exposure can cause standard deck paints to crack and peel within a single season. Once the protective coating fails, moisture quickly reaches the structural plywood, leading to **dry rot** and expensive structural repairs.

Our restoration process is designed to break this cycle, providing a durable, long-term barrier that protects your investment and enhances your home’s aesthetic.

## Our Coastal Restoration Process

We utilize a proven, multi-layer system from industry-leading manufacturers like **Westcoat and Sika** to ensure your deck is truly "San Diego Proof":

1.  **Comprehensive Cleaning & Prep:** Removing all old, failing coatings and surface contaminants to ensure a perfect bond.
2.  **Structural Assessment:** Identifying and repairing any "hidden" dry rot in the plywood or joists.
3.  **Detailing & Flashing:** Installing or repairing metal flashing at walls and edges—the most common points of failure in coastal decks.
4.  **Base Waterproofing:** Applying a high-strength, seamless membrane reinforced with metal lath or fiberglass.
5.  **UV-Resistant Topcoat:** Finishing with a premium, non-slip topcoat in your choice of color, designed specifically for high UV and salt exposure.
