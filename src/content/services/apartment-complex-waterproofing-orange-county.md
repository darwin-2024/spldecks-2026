## Protecting Residents and Structural Assets

In the multi-family sector, elevated walkways and resident balconies are more than just amenities—they are critical structural assets. Constant foot traffic and exposure to the coastal Orange County environment can lead to moisture intrusion, causing costly rot and safety hazards. Our systems provide a durable, seamless barrier that protects your investment and ensures tenant safety.

## Specialized Multi-Unit Deck Systems

We recognize that waterproofing an occupied apartment building requires a different approach than a single-family home. Our team designs systems specifically for high-use environments, focusing on:

- **High-Durability Walkway Membranes:** Preventing leaks into lower-level units and stopping structural decay in its tracks.
- **Balcony Restoration:** Providing residents with a fresh, safe, and aesthetically pleasing outdoor space.
- **Common Area Sealing:** Protecting elevated courtyards and the vital structural components beneath them.
