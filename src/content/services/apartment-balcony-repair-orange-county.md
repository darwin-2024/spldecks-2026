# Apartment Complex Balcony Repair & Restoration in Orange County

**SPL Decks** is the premier choice for **apartment balcony repair** and restoration throughout **Orange County**. We understand the unique challenges faced by property managers and HOA boards when maintaining elevated exterior elements in high-density environments.

## The Critical Importance of Balcony Maintenance

In Orange County, coastal salt air and seasonal rains can take a heavy toll on cantilevered and supported balconies. When the waterproof membrane fails, moisture quickly reaches the structural plywood and framing, leading to **dry rot** and compromised safety. 

Our team doesn't just "patch" leaks—we address the root cause, restoring structural integrity and applying industrial-grade coatings that stand up to years of use.

## SB-721 and SB-326 Compliance Specialists

California's "Balcony Laws" (SB-721 for apartments and SB-326 for HOAs) require regular inspections and timely repairs of elevated structural elements. SPL Decks works closely with inspectors and property managers to execute necessary repairs efficiently.

- **Structural Reinforcement:** Replacing rotted joists and plywood to meet safety standards.
- **Waterproofing Upgrades:** Installing modern, multi-layer systems that prevent future moisture intrusion.
- **Documentation:** Providing the necessary proof of repair for compliance records.

## Our Restoration Process

We utilize a proven, multi-step process to ensure every balcony we touch is safe, waterproof, and beautiful:

1.  **Demolition & Inspection:** Carefully removing old coatings to inspect the substrate for damage.
2.  **Structural Repair:** Removing dry-rotted wood and installing new, high-grade structural plywood and flashing.
3.  **Base Membrane:** Applying a primary waterproofing layer, often reinforced with metal lath or fiberglass.
4.  **Detailing:** Precision flashing at walls, doors, and edges to prevent "hidden" leaks.
5.  **Finish Coat:** Applying a durable, UV-resistant topcoat with a non-slip texture in the client's choice of color.
